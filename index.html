About Me

Gregory Brown is an Atlanta based Full Stack developer and entrepreneur. He has consulted for an array of businesses from Non-Profit organizations to educational institutions. An Atlanta native, Greg graduated from Morehouse College with a bachelor of arts in African-American Studies. He has a passion for technology and has been in the field in varies roles over the last 15 years. Greg is currently working a start-up with HBCU Pro.
